The premise

Bali is not one place.

It's an archipelago of moods. The unhurried south, where the cliffs drop into reefs and you wake up to ocean breeze. The cultural middle, where surf, design studios and night markets share one neighbourhood. The hills above Ubud, the rice terraces, the temple festivals.

We chose two of these moods, and built differently in each. Not the same product in two postcodes — two products, two architectures, two daily rhythms. Held together by one design language and one operator.

What both projects share

One studio. One builder. One operator.

01 · Architecture

Studio Identity Designers — Jakarta.

One design language, two readings. Restrained materials, generous shade, indoor-outdoor flow — and a refusal of the postcard-Bali cliché. The villas read like a private estate; the apartments read like a tropical city home.

02 · Hospitality

MyResorts — hotel-grade hospitality.

A single operator runs both properties. Pre-arrival concierge, daily housekeeping, private chef on request, a rental programme for owners who want yield. You buy a home; you don't acquire an operations problem.
