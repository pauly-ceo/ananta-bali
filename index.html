The premise

Bali absorbs quiet design.

01 · Design

Studio Identity Designers — Jakarta.

One design language across both projects: restrained materials, generous shade, indoor-outdoor flow, and a refusal of the postcard-Bali cliché. The villas read like a private estate; the apartments read like a tropical city home.

02 · Operator

MyResorts — hotel-grade hospitality.

A single operator runs both properties: pre-arrival concierge, daily housekeeping, private chef on request, rental program for owners who want yield. You buy a home; you don't acquire an operations problem.

Both projects are built by Thierra, our group's construction arm — 1.3 bn CZK annual turnover, twenty years of premium residential. Same standards, same team, same accountability across Desa Kutuh and Canggu.
